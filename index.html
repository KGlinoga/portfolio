About Me

I've worked in many different contexts, from jewelry sales to whole animal butchery. But no matter the context, two things I always bring to the table are that I don’t cut corners at the expense of the product and I always look for the best, most efficient way to achieve our high standards.


As a Filipino American I am passionate about supporting an informed population participating in democracy. That’s why a portion of my salary will be donated to Rappler, the independent journalism organization that fights the rampant state-sponsored misinformation campaigns in the Philippines, lead by Nobel-Peace Prize winner, Maria Ressa. I hope to find an employer who matches donations so that we can genuinely support democracy in the world without violence.

If your company wants exceptional work and is cool supporting a digital nomad and social justice, OR you're BIPOC/WOC in tech, please drop me a line - I’d love to connect!
